Budgies(Budgerigar) are native to Australia. Budgies are very social creatures and stick together in large flocks. Budgies live in arid open areas, like savanas, grass lands, and scrubs. They feed on small seeds and crops. Budgies can mate at any time of the year but typically like too nest after rainfall to ensure a ready food supply. Budgies are cool.
