Rosa parks

The Mother of the Civil Rights Movement

Rosa Parks was a social rights activist for African Americans in the early 1900s .
She refused to give up her seat on a bus to a white man when that was the law back then and so she got arrested for it.
She helped start a boycott for busses to show that, that was not right and that they should be equal.
